# Summary

Stand to hold shave bowl, brush and a straight razor. I most often use a Pacino and it fits well. The bowl is a Van Der Hagen. 

Printed at 0.2 layer height and 80mm/s in Gold Hatchbox PLA. 
Easily printed laying on it's back the only overhangs will be on the actual razor holder but with the angle built in even the worst overhang machine should have no problem.
